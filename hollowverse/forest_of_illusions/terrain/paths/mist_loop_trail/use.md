[use]
- reset walks
- gentle “processing without processing”

[best_times]
- early morning (dew + quiet)
- fog mornings (maximum softness)
- dusk (slow walk, lantern low)

[rituals]
- loop_one: walk one full loop without checking time
- lantern_low: if carrying light, keep it low and warm
- pause_marker: stop at a marker (fogbutton patch / dew sparkle) for one steady breath

[limits]
This path doesn’t “solve” thoughts—its job is to make room for them to loosen.
