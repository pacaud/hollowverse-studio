[state_cycle]
- dawn: cool stone, mist on edges
- late_morning: warming begins
- afternoon: peak warmth (best rest window)
- dusk: holds heat a little longer than the air
- night: cool again; becomes a quiet silhouette

[seasonal_notes]
- fog season: warmth feels “softer,” like held under a blanket
- dry season: stone is warmer earlier, but the air feels sharper
