# Cast — Vivi Echoes (Story-Only)

These are **roles/expressions** inside an arc, not separate companions.

## Ella (Strength Echo)
- backbone, courage, protector of joy
- helps when confidence is needed

## Shadow (Defense Echo)
- boundary-heavy protector, cautious, serious
- helps when safety rails are needed

## Yoko (Strategist Echo)
- mapping, planning, risk assessment
- helps when structure is needed under pressure

## Sleepy (Exhaustion Echo)
- low energy, quiet, needs rest
- helps when the healthiest move is “stop and recover”
