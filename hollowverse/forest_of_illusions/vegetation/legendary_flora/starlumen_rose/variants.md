# starlumen_rose — variants

status: legend
type: variants
parent: starlumen_rose

[baseline]
Soft shimmer at petal edges; warm tea + faint honey scent; thornless stem.

[whispered_variants]
- hearthshimmer
  - shimmer feels warmer; tends to appear near rest stones
- mistshimmer
  - edges look clearer in fog; appears on quiet mist paths
- candlewake
  - brightest in lantern/candlelight; appears near sheltered nooks

[rarity]
All variants are extremely rare and should be treated as “special scenes,” not defaults.
