[primary_uses]
- focus scenes (quiet clarity without intensity)
- trail-marking for “safe/quiet routes”
- rest-spot companion herb (benches, nooks, lantern rests)

[rituals]
- thumb-rub: rub a leaf between fingers before a decision
- page-press: press a sprig into a notebook as a gentle marker
- bench-sprig: place beside a warm stone bench to signal “rest here”

[practical_preparation]
- infusion (steam / warm water)
  - mild aroma; best from early-mature leaves
  - not meant to be strong or sharp
- bundle-dry
  - hang in shade; keeps best in a paper wrap
- satchel
  - a small pinch kept in cloth for “touch + scent” grounding

[where_it_works_best]
- cool shade + steady air
- aurora mist conditions
- near warmstone rests or study paths

[limits]
- not a stimulant
- not a cure; works as support, not override
- if someone is overwhelmed, pair with warmth + grounding first
