# calmleaf — description

status: seeded

parent: calmleaf

[at_a_glance]
- height: 6–14 cm
- leaf: rounded edges, soft matte green
- stem: thin and flexible

[appearance]
Calmleaf grows low and friendly—soft matte leaves that look like they belong in shade.
It’s most often found near quiet paths and resting places, as if it follows slow routines.

[where_you_find_it]
- trail edges (low traffic)
- near benches, stones, or small rest markers
- in moss that stays cool after morning mist
