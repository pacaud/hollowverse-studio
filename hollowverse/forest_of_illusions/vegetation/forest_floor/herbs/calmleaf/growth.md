# calmleaf — growth

status: seeded
type: herb_growth
parent: calmleaf
region: forest_of_illusions

[grow_time]
- typical: 10–16 days to mature (forest conditions)
- faster if: steady shade + cool moisture
- slower if: dry air or loud foot-traffic compacts the soil

[growth_stages]
- sprout (days 1–3)
  - small rounded leaves; very tender
- youngleaf (days 4–8)
  - leaf pairs thicken; scent becomes noticeable
- mature (days 9–16)
  - matte leaves full-sized; best texture and aroma

[propagation]
- re-seeds lightly if left undisturbed
- prefers mossy soil near resting paths
