# hushwort — growth

status: rumor
type: herb_growth
parent: hushwort
region: forest_of_illusions

[grow_time]
- slow: 10–18 days from sprout to full leaf
- prefers: shaded calm pockets, low disturbance

[growth_stages]
- hushsprout (days 1–4)
  - tiny pale-green points near leaf litter
- quietleaf (days 5–12)
  - thin muted leaves; still hard to notice
- inward_mature (days 13–18)
  - fuller cluster; “muted air” effect feels clearest nearby

[notes]
If the area stays loud or rushed, it often fails to establish.
