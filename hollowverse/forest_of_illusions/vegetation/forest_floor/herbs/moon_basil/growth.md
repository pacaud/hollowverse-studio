# moon_basil — growth

status: seeded
type: herb_growth
parent: moon_basil
region: forest_of_illusions

[grow_time]
- typical: 8–13 days to mature (forest conditions)
- faster if: cool nights + steady mist
- slower if: harsh sun or dry air

[growth_stages]
- sprout (days 1–3)
  - small paired leaves; prefers soft moisture
- youngleaf (days 4–6)
  - dark green deepens; sheen begins
- night-open (days 7–9)
  - leaves open more fully at dusk/night
- mature (days 10–13)
  - stable aroma; best for gentle comfort uses

[notes]
Moon basil tolerates daylight, but it “belongs” to the evening.
