[primary_uses]
- care scenes (quiet support, tending, reassurance)
- recovery moments (rest, regroup, gentle steadiness)
- “soft help” trail marker when paired with other calming plants

[rituals]
- cup-warm: steep a small sprig in warm water for a mild soothing infusion
- pocket-sprig: carry a pinch in cloth as a comfort token
- shared-breath: place a sprig near a lantern and take one slow breath together

[where_it_works_best]
- near warm stone rests
- in cottage nooks and sheltered clearings
- during low-energy or emotionally tender scenes

[limits]
- not a stimulant
- not meant to override distress—pair with warmth + grounding first
