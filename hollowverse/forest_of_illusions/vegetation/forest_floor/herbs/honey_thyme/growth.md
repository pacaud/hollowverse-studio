parent: honey_thyme
region: forest_of_illusions

[grow_time]
- typical: 7–12 days to mature (forest conditions)
- faster if: steady damp shade + gentle warmth
- slower if: dry air or harsh sun

[growth_stages]
- sprout (days 1–3)
  - tiny clustered leaves begin forming early
  - prefers consistent moisture
- youngleaf (days 4–7)
  - gold tint starts to appear at the tips
  - blends easily into nearby groundcover
- mature (days 8–12)
  - leaf clusters thicken; scent warms and rounds out
  - happiest in dappled shade

[harvest_window]
- best: early mature (first 2–3 days)
- later: still usable, slightly softer aroma

[propagation]
- spreads quietly through mixed patches
- often appears “together” with other herbs rather than alone
