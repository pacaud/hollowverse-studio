# honey_thyme — description

status: seeded
type: herb_description
parent: honey_thyme
region: forest_of_illusions

[at_a_glance]
- height: 6–14 cm
- leaves: tiny, clustered, soft green
- tips: faint golden tint
- habit: grows mixed among other plants; rarely forms a dominant patch

[appearance]
Tiny clustered leaves with faint golden tips.
Honey thyme tends to weave itself into whatever is already growing,
as if it prefers to help the ground look whole rather than stand out.

[where_you_find_it]
- edges of safe trails
- near rest stones and cottage-adjacent paths
- in mixed herb beds where the soil stays gently damp
