# dewlace — growth

status: seeded
type: flower_growth
parent: dewlace
region: forest_of_illusions

[grow_time]
- typical: 6–12 days from sprout to first bloom
- best when: cool nights + steady moisture

[growth_stages]
- sprout (day 1–3)
  - tiny green point in moss or soft soil
- bud (day 4–7)
  - closed white bud, easy to miss
- dew-bloom (day 8–10)
  - dawn bloom where the “lace edge” appears
- fade (day 11–12)
  - petals soften and vanish into the floor

[notes]
Dewlace is more visible by dew than by color—its “show” is the morning.
