# ribbon_thistle — growth

status: seeded
type: flower_growth
parent: ribbon_thistle
region: forest_of_illusions

[bloom_time]
- most often: midday light
- can appear: late morning to early afternoon
- fades: toward dusk

[stages]
- bud
  - tight crown; guards feel sharp
- ribbon-bloom
  - soft filaments show; center turns gentle
- seed-wisp
  - filaments thin and drift; the “ribbon” look becomes airy

[where_it_takes_hold]
- path edges
- worn trails that need soft boundaries
- sunny breaks in canopy
