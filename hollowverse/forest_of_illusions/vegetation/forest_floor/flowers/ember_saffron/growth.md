# ember_saffron — growth

status: seeded
type: flower_growth
parent: ember_saffron
region: forest_of_illusions

[grow_time]
- typical: 10–16 days from sprout to first bloom
- faster if: dappled sun + warm stones nearby
- slower if: heavy shade or frequent cold mist

[growth_stages]
- sprout (days 1–3)
  - small paired leaves, deep green
- leafset (days 4–8)
  - low, tidy cluster of leaves; stem begins to form
- bud (days 9–12)
  - tight cup bud, color begins warming at the tip
- bloom (days 13–16)
  - opens late afternoon; closes slightly overnight
- fade
  - color softens first, then the cup relaxes and drops

[where_it_thrives]
- near fallen logs and sunlit stones
- edges of clearings where late-day light reaches the floor
