# starpetal — growth

status: seeded
type: flower_growth
parent: starpetal
region: forest_of_illusions

[bloom_window]
- late afternoon → early night
- closes gently by deep night; opens again as the light softens the next day

[growth_stages]
- sprout (days 1–3)
  - low green start, hard to spot in grass
- bud (days 4–7)
  - small pale bud; warmth gathers at the center
- starbloom (days 8–14)
  - petals open; center brightens at dusk
- rest (after bloom window)
  - petals close; plant holds steady without looking wilted

[conditions]
- prefers: cool soil, light shade, familiar footpaths
- dislikes: harsh midday sun and very dry air

[propagation]
- spreads in small clusters over time
- tends to return in the same “welcome home” spots year after year
