[visual]
- shape: small rounded “button” caps
- color: pale gray-cream, sometimes with a faint pearl tint
- fog effect: looks brighter/clearer when the air is misty

[scent]
- mild clean dampness (moss + cool air)
- strongest right after fog break

[touch]
- cap: smooth, slightly cool
- stem: delicate; breaks easily if handled roughly

[sound]
- none; they’re “quiet” in the way snow is quiet

[notes]
If you need a one-line sensory summary for pulls:
“pale buttons that glow slightly brighter in fog, smelling like clean damp moss.”
