# fogbutton — description

status: seeded
type: mushroom_description
parent: fogbutton
region: forest_of_illusions

[at_a_glance]
- height: 2–6 cm
- cap: rounded, “button-like”
- stem: thin and fragile
- clusters: often scattered in small constellations through moss

[appearance]
Fogbuttons look like tiny pale buttons sewn into the forest floor.
They’re most noticeable when the air turns milky—fog seems to outline them,
making them appear cleaner and brighter than the moss around them.

[where_you_find_it]
- moss beds near quiet trails
- shaded clearings with cool air pockets
- stream edges where mist lingers
