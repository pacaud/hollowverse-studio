parent: fogbutton
region: forest_of_illusions

[grow_time]
- typical: 4–9 days from first pin to spore fade
- fastest if: repeated fog mornings + cool shade
- slower if: dry air or hot direct sun

[growth_stages]
- pin (day 1–2)
  - tiny pale bumps in moss; easy to miss
- button (day 3–5)
  - classic rounded cap; “fogbutton” look
- open-cap (day 6–7)
  - cap loosens slightly; edges soften
- spore-fade (day 8–9)
  - dims, collapses, returns to the moss

[spawning_conditions]
- prefers: thick moss, damp soil, sheltered shade
- trigger: fog-heavy mornings or lingering mist pockets

[propagation]
- spreads quietly through mycelium under moss beds
- returns in the same patch when conditions repeat
