# inkveil — growth

status: seeded
type: mushroom_growth
parent: inkveil
region: forest_of_illusions

[grow_time]
- typical: 4–8 days from first pin to spore fade
- fastest if: cool shade + damp ground + calm air
- slower if: dry wind or strong sun

[growth_stages]
- pin (day 1–2)
  - tiny dark bumps in moss or leaf litter
- veilcap (day 3–5)
  - thin cap forms; bluish sheen appears
- foldveil (day 6–7)
  - underside folds deepen; cap looks more “layered”
- spore-fade (day 8)
  - collapses softly back into the floor

[propagation]
- returns in the same patches after repeated damp, quiet nights
