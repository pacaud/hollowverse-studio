# mooncap — growth

status: seeded
type: mushroom_growth
parent: mooncap
region: forest_of_illusions

[grow_time]
- typical: 5–10 days from first pin to spore fade
- fastest if: cool nights + damp shade
- slower if: dry air or strong sun

[growth_stages]
- pin (day 1–2)
  - small pale nubs in moss or leaf litter
- capped (day 3–6)
  - rounded pale cap; sheen becomes noticeable at night
- mature (day 7–8)
  - cap flattens slightly; sheen looks more even
- spore-fade (day 9–10)
  - dims and softens back into the floor

[spawning_conditions]
- prefers: thick moss, cool shade, steady dampness
- trigger: calm nights after misty mornings

[propagation]
- returns in the same patches when conditions repeat
