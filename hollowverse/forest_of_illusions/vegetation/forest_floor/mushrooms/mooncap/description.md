# mooncap — description

status: seeded
type: mushroom_description
parent: mooncap
region: forest_of_illusions

[appearance]
Pale caps with a faint, soft sheen in darkness.
They do not shine brightly—more like a calm reflection.

[placement]
Mooncaps often appear in small clusters near paths that feel safe at night,
especially where moss thickens and the air stays cool.
