[light]
- dim, moonlike shimmer (never a strong glow)
- most visible at dusk + deep shade
- fog can make the edges look clearer

[scent]
- cool earth
- clean damp wood

[touch]
- cap: smooth, slightly cool
- stem: firmer than most moss-floor mushrooms

[sound]
- none; the “sound” is the quiet you notice around it
