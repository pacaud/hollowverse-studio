# mist_pine — growth

status: seeded
type: tree_growth
parent: mist_pine
region: forest_of_illusions

[growth_notes]
Mist pine grows like a normal canopy pine, but it prefers places where fog returns often.

[stages]
- sapling
  - dark needles; stays low and dense
- young
  - begins “fog-drink” look: trunk darkens slightly in mist
- mature
  - tall canopy presence; steadies the air underneath

[conditions]
- thrives: cool shade, frequent mist, steady airflow
- struggles: prolonged dry heat and direct midday sun
