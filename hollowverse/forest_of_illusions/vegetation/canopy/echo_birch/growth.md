# echo_birch — growth

status: seeded
type: tree_growth
parent: echo_birch
region: forest_of_illusions

[at_a_glance]
- height: 6–14 m (canopy), slender trunk
- bark: pale with cool ridges
- leaves: thin, paperlike; high “page-rustle” even in light wind

[seasonal_cycle]
- spring: new leaves come in very light, almost translucent
- summer: steady hush; strongest scent of cool bark + mineral air
- autumn: leaves turn parchment-gold and fall in soft drifts along paths
- winter: trunk shimmer is most visible in moonlight; branches look ink-thin

[conditions]
- prefers: cool air pockets, mineral soil, gentle night light
- dislikes: hot direct sun for long stretches (leaf-rustle goes quiet)
