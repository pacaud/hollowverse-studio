# mistblue_bush — growth

status: seeded
type: plant_growth
parent: mistblue_bush
region: forest_of_illusions

[grow_time]
- flowering: late spring → early summer (forest conditions)
- fruiting: early summer → fog season
- peak berries: during cool mornings and fog pockets

[growth_stages]
- sprout
  - small clustered leaves close to the ground
- leafy_bush
  - low rounded form; prefers shade and damp air
- berry_set
  - pale berries form; “dusty” look appears as the skin matures
- ripe
  - berries soften slightly; flavor stays mild and watery-clean
- fade
  - berries drop and the bush returns to quiet leaf growth

[notes]
Mistblue favors still air, mossy soil, and the edges of mist_pine groves.
