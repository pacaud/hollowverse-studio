A compact shrub with matte leaves that seem to absorb light.
It creates privacy without making a space feel closed-in.

[placement]
Often found framing rest areas—near benches, small clearings, or quiet nooks.
It tends to grow where a boundary is needed, but a wall would feel unkind.
