# hushbrush — growth

status: seeded
type: shrub_growth
parent: hushbrush
region: forest_of_illusions
layer: understory

[grow_rate]
- slow-steady; spreads by patient thickening rather than fast runners

[stages]
- sprig (weeks 1–4)
  - small matte leaves; prefers shade and consistent moisture
- young_shrub (months 2–6)
  - begins forming a rounded "privacy" shape; sound-muffling starts
- mature (6+ months)
  - compact, dense leaf clusters; strongest boundary feel

[conditions]
- likes: cool shade, mossy soil, sheltered air
- dislikes: harsh direct sun and very dry wind

[maintenance]
- self-trims naturally; stays compact unless disturbed
