# lantern_moss_fern — growth

status: seeded
type: fern_growth
parent: lantern_moss_fern
region: forest_of_illusions

[grow_time]
- typical: 12–20 days to a mature tuft (forest conditions)
- faster if: steady shade + damp moss beds
- slower if: dry air or harsh sunlight

[growth_stages]
- beadspore (days 1–3)
  - tiny specks near moss, barely visible
- curlsprout (days 4–8)
  - small curled fronds; beads begin to form
- frondlet (days 9–14)
  - low fern shape; shimmer becomes noticeable at dusk
- mature tuft (days 15–20)
  - fuller spread; beads catch light like warm dust

[propagation]
- spreads quietly through mossy soil and sheltered hollows
- returns in the same “safe corner” patches when conditions repeat
