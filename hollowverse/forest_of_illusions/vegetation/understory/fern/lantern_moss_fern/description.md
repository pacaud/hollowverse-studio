[description]
A low fern with tiny moss-like beads along its stems.
In dim light the beads catch a warm shimmer—never bright.

[where_you_find_it]
- sheltered hollows where wind doesn’t reach
- mossy roots at the base of old trees
- dim corners near warmstone rests or tucked benches
