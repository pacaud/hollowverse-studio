# mist_pine_sapling — lore

status: seeded
type: lore_profile
parent: mist_pine_sapling
region: forest_of_illusions

[common_belief]
Mist Pine Saplings appear when a place is becoming calmer over time—
like the forest is committing to gentleness there.

[trail_lore]
- Shows up near rest spots and quiet corners.

[omens]
- saplings in a cluster: a safe-feeling area is forming
- lone sapling near a bench: the forest wants the bench to be found
- sapling needles drying: the corner has been too noisy or too dry
