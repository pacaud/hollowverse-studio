# mist_pine_sapling — description

status: seeded
type: sapling_description
parent: mist_pine_sapling
region: forest_of_illusions

[description]
A young pine that gathers fog close to its needles like a tiny blanket.

[appearance_notes]
- height: 18–60 cm (most common)
- needles: soft-looking, fine, and slightly darker at the tips
- fog behavior: mist clings around it longer than nearby plants

[where_you_find_it]
- understory pockets with consistent moisture
- near rest spots and quiet corners
- along low-wind bends in trails
