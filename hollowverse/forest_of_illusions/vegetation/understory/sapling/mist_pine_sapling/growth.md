# mist_pine_sapling — growth

status: seeded
type: growth_profile
parent: mist_pine_sapling
region: forest_of_illusions

[grow_time]
- sprout to sapling: 3–8 weeks
- sapling to young tree: 1–3 seasons (slow and steady)
- best conditions: cool shade, fog pockets, damp soil

[growth_stages]
- sprout
  - thin green needle tuft, easy to miss in moss
- sapling
  - fog begins to cling longer around needles
- young pine
  - bark firms up; scent becomes clearer after rain

[notes]
Mist Pine Saplings grow slowly on purpose—
they prefer places that stay calm for a long time.
