# shade_cabbage — growth

status: seeded
type: plant_growth
parent: shade_cabbage
region: forest_of_illusions

[grow_time]
- sprout → mature: 10–18 days (cool shade conditions)

[growth_stages]
- sprout
  - small curled leaves close to the ground
- bowl-leaf
  - leaves widen and begin to hold droplets
- mature
  - full “rain bowls” form; strongest in steady shade
- rest
  - during dry spells, leaves flatten and turn quieter until rain returns

[conditions]
- prefers: shade + damp soil + slow air
- dislikes: harsh sun and dry wind
