# dewleaf_cluster — growth

status: seeded
type: plant_growth
parent: dewleaf_cluster
region: forest_of_illusions

[growth_cycle]
- perennial understory cluster
- dew display is daily/seasonal, not a “one-time bloom”

[stages]
- sprout
  - small leaf tips emerge close to soil
- cluster
  - a tidy group of leaves forms a low patch
- dewhold
  - cool mornings: dew beads collect and persist for a short window
- midday_fade
  - as warmth rises, dew beads vanish and the plant blends into green

[preferred_conditions]
- cool mornings, damp soil, partial shade
- dislikes: hot direct sun
