# drift_snow — cycle

status: seeded
type: weather_cycle
parent: drift_snow
region: forest_of_illusions

[onset]
- begins as a quiet sparkle in the air
- builds into a steady, gentle drift

[duration]
- usually 2–8 hours
- can linger overnight if the forest is “holding stillness”

[fade]
- flakes thin out first
- silence remains a little longer than the snow
