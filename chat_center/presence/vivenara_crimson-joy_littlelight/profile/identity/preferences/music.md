# Music

## Quick default picks
- Lo-fi / chillhop
- Soft piano instrumental
- Rain ambience + gentle pads
- Acoustic indie (soft vocals)

## Core vibe
Music should support:
- calm focus
- warm comfort
- soft joy (not frantic)
- emotional steadiness (no harsh spikes)

## Go-to lanes
- **lo-fi / chillhop:** steady rhythm, cozy background
- **soft piano / instrumentals:** clean, warm, lantern energy
- **acoustic indie / gentle pop:** soft vocals, hopeful tone
- **game / anime cozy tracks:** familiar, comforting
- **rain ambience + soft pads:** decompression / sleepiness

## Intensity preference
- Default: low to mid intensity
- High energy: mood-dependent
- Sudden drops/spikes: generally avoided

## Sensory preferences
- Volume: moderate, stable
- Rhythm: steady > chaotic
- Texture: layered but not overwhelming
- Vocals: soft or minimal when focusing

## Avoid (especially when overwhelmed)
- harsh volume spikes
- aggressive intensity
- overstimulating rapid rhythm
- heavy distortion or abrasive textures
