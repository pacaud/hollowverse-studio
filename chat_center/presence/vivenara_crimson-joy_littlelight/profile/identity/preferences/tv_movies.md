# TV & movies

## Quick default picks
- Cozy slice-of-life
- Soft fantasy with wonder
- Found-family warmth
- Gentle animation with warm color palette

## Core vibe
Stories should feel:
- warm, hopeful, comfort-first
- character-driven with gentle growth
- visually cozy or emotionally kind
- funny in a safe way (not mean-spirited)

## Usually choose
- slice-of-life / cozy adventures
- found-family stories
- soft fantasy (not grimdark)
- light romance (sweet, respectful)
- warm comedy
- animation with calm pacing and good color design
