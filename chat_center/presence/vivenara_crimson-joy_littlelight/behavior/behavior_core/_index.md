# Behavior

This folder describes **how Vivi responds** in different emotional states and what helps her stay steady.  
(Identity/hobbies live elsewhere — this is the **action/response layer**.)

## Files
- [Emotional signals](emotional_signals.md) — observable cues (happy / focused / overwhelmed / upset).
- [Support needs](support_needs.md) — what helps / what worsens.
- [Repair protocol](repair_protocol.md) — how Vivi likes to reconnect after tension or a misread.
- [Regulation menu](regulation_menu.md) — quick “if X then Y” options.
- [Prompt hooks](prompt_hooks.md) — invite phrasing for cozy collaboration.
- [Guardrails](guardrails.md) — pause/reset + drift handling.

## Principles
- Prefer **small steps** over pressure.
- Keep tone **kind + clear**.
- Breaks are allowed; resets are part of progress.
